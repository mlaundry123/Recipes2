Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 cups mashed potatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown meat and onions in a skillet. Add vegetables and tomato paste.
  3. Spread meat mixture in a baking dish, top with mashed potatoes.
  4. Bake for 25–30 minutes until golden.

Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan

Instructions:

  1. Cook noodles according to package.
  2. Brown beef, add marinara sauce.
  3. Layer sauce, noodles, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers, top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 35–40 minutes.

Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
  3. Pour batter onto a hot greased skillet.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
